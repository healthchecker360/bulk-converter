Our mission

FileForge was created with one goal: to give everyone access to professional-grade file tools without the friction of subscriptions, watermarks, or account creation. Every PDF tool, image compressor, and format converter we build is free — period.

Most of our tools work entirely in your browser, meaning your files never leave your device. For tools that require server processing, all files are encrypted in transit and permanently deleted from our servers within 1 hour.

Privacy first

We take your privacy seriously. FileForge does not sell your data, does not use your files for training, and does not share information with third parties. All file processing is protected by 256-bit SSL encryption.
